My Home Town

Kanyakumari is a coastal town in the state of Tamil Nadu on India's southern tip. It is the meeting point of three oceans-the Bay of Bengal, the Arabian Sea and the Indian Ocean. The place is famous for its beautiful views of sunrise and sunset over the waters.
